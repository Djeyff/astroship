FAQ

Common questions

Does my team need to install anything? +

No. You add Rewa's number to your WhatsApp group or scan a QR code once. No app, no extension, no IT ticket needed.

Can Rewa read my messages? +

No. All data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM using your unique key before reaching our database. We physically cannot read your content.

Does it work with voice notes in groups? +

Yes — that's Rewa's core feature. Every voice note in every connected group is automatically transcribed and searchable. No manual action needed from anyone.

What languages are supported? +

50+ languages including Spanish, English, French, Portuguese, Arabic and more. Mixed-language voice notes handled automatically.

What happens when I go over my limits? +

You get notified at 80% usage. Overages are auto-billed at transparent per-unit rates. No hard shutoffs — your team keeps working.

Is this compliant with WhatsApp's terms? +

Yes. Rewa uses the official WhatsApp Business API via Meta Cloud API and Twilio. All group members are notified that Rewa is present.
